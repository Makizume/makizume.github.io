  • Business Name: Studio Supreme

  • Contact Information: For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Page .

2. Information We Collect and How We Use It

We collect information primarily to provide and improve our Service. The information we collect is limited to the following:

A. Advertising-Related Information

  • We use third-party advertising services, including AdMob and Unity Ads, to display ads within our apps. These services may automatically collect:
    • Device information (e.g., advertising identifiers, device type, and operating system version)
    • Usage data related to ad interactions
  • Purpose: To serve and optimize advertisements within our Service

B. Feature-Specific Information

  • Our apps may include features such as leaderboards and social sharing functions (e.g., X, Instagram). When you use these features, we may collect:
    • Information you provide directly (e.g., usernames, scores, or content you choose to share)
    • Data generated from your interactions with these features
  • Purpose:
    • To facilitate app functionality, such as displaying leaderboards and enabling social sharing
    • To enhance and improve our Service

C. Information Provided Directly by You

  • If you choose to contact us or provide feedback through our Service, we may collect the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address.
  • Use: Responding to your inquiries and improving our Service based on your feedback

Note: Except for the data collected via the advertising services and the optional leaderboard/social sharing features, we do not automatically collect other personal information.

3. Data Retention and Security

  • Data Retention: We will retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by applicable law. When your data is no longer needed, it will be deleted or anonymized.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • Third-Party Advertising Services: We share only the necessary information with our advertising partners (AdMob, Unity Ads) to display ads and optimize ad performance. These partners have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.
  • Social Sharing and Leaderboards: When you use features such as social sharing or participate in leaderboards, some of your information may be shared with external platforms (e.g., X, Instagram). Please refer to the privacy policies of those platforms for details on how your information is managed.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Correction: You may request to review, update, or correct the information we hold about you.
  • Opt-Out: If you have concerns about the collection and use of your information for advertising, please review the settings provided by the respective advertising platforms.
  • Contact: For any questions or requests regarding your personal information, please contact us through our Contact Page .

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our Service (for example, on our Notion page), and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Additional Information

If you have any further questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please visit our Contact Page .
